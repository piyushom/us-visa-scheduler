U.S. Visa Appointment Scheduler

A Chrome extension that monitors the AIS website and books an earlier U.S. visa appointment automatically — so you don't have to keep refreshing.

Add to Chrome — It's Free
🔍

Automatic Monitoring

Scans AIS for available dates at your chosen interval — no manual refreshing needed.

📅

Auto-Booking

When an earlier slot appears within your date range, the extension books it for you instantly.

🔔

Session Alerts

Get notified when your AIS session expires so you can log back in and restart the scheduler.

🔒

Your Session Only

Works entirely within your own logged-in browser session. No passwords or credentials ever stored.

How It Works

1

Install the extension and open AIS

Navigate to your appointment page on ais.usvisa-info.com and select your consular location.

2

Set your target date range

Enter the earliest and latest dates you'd accept. The scheduler only acts within this range.

3

Click Start Scheduler

The extension runs in the background, checking for available slots at your chosen scan interval.

4

Get your earlier appointment

When a slot opens within your range, the extension books it automatically and notifies you.
